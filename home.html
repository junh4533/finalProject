Welcome to SEP

Introduction

Welcome to the SEP website. Here you will find all information about our program. Our goal is for students to learn about all kinds of ways to be well ready for a job in a field that requires computer science We want these students to have a career in software as there are many seats that are unfilled and the demand for software developers are increasing.

What is SEP??

SEP is a computer science program established by NYC to have students become familiar with many concepts in the field of software engineering such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The more students become educated in the field in computer science, the more they are able to land a job in that career. In order for students to become knowledgeable of software engineering, they will learn many skills and concepts

How to join HSTAT SEP as a student

If you want to join SEP at HSTAT, you want to make sure that you have the readiness to learn new and challenging things. We do not accept anyone who refuses to work.
