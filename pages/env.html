The SEP Community

Our classroom is more like a community rather than a traditional classroom. Students are often encouraged to explore on their own, rather than simply taking notes and memorizing for the next test. When they get stuck, their neighbors will glady assist them.

What's so awesome about this class?

  • Almost no homework at all!
  • Rarely any tests/quizzes :)
  • You're allowed to talk in class!
  • Access to the internet for more resources
